Welcome to Electronics Engineering Department

We strive to be a model institute for providing academic and skill excellence in Electronics Engineering. Our department offers a Diploma in Electronics Engineering, focusing on cutting-edge technologies, practical training, and innovation.

With state-of-the-art labs and experienced faculty, we prepare students for successful careers in electronics, embedded systems, communication, and more.

About the Department

The Department of Electronics Engineering at Maharaja's Technological Institute, Thrissur, aims to impart quality teaching-learning experiences with a modern curriculum.

Vision: To be a model institute for academic and skill excellence in Electronics Engineering.

Mission: Provide quality education, enhance training, promote industry-institute interaction, and foster entrepreneurship development.

The department offers a 3-year Diploma program with hands-on experience in circuits, microcontrollers, VLSI, and communication systems.
